## Open-source

[LifeOmic] is committed to open-source.  Our platform is powered by it and our team contributes upstream whenever possible.  Our projects follow the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) and will enforce this standard in our public channels.  We welcome forks, PRs, and feedback through our projects.
